Pre-Market Routine
Run through this every morning before your first trade. Discipline creates consistency.
📰 Before Open (8–9:30 AM)
Read pre-market news (earnings, macro, Fed events)
Check futures — S&P, Nasdaq, Dow trending up or down?
Scan for gappers (stocks up/down 3%+ pre-market)
Identify 2–3 stocks for your watchlist today
Set your max loss limit for the day (3% of account)
Mark key support & resistance on your target tickers
🎯 During & After Trading
Wait the first 15 min — let the open volatility settle
Only trade your pre-planned setups — no impulse entries
Set stop-loss BEFORE entering every trade
Log each trade: entry, exit, reason, emotional state
Stop trading if you hit your daily loss limit
Review all trades after close — what worked, what didn't?
